## MARKET SEGMENTS

### Segment A: Gastronomie (Quick-Win, Months 1–3)
- **Market size:** 250–300 businesses in Lübeck
- **Target profile:** Hotels, restaurants, cafés with 5–30 staff
- **Avg deal size:** €500–1,500/month retainer
- **Primary pain:** Staff scheduling chaos, no-shows, manual reservation handling
- **Playbook:** `.cowork/segments/segment_a_gastro.md`

### Segment B: Handwerk (Long-term Play, Months 2–9)
- **Market size:** 700+ businesses (HWK Lübeck network)
- **Target profile:** Electricians, plumbers, painters with 3–15 technicians
- **Avg deal size:** €400–800/month retainer
- **Primary pain:** Paper-based dispatch, delayed invoicing, no customer feedback loop
- **Gateway:** HWK Lübeck partnership (see `luebeck/sales/hwk_partnership_pitch_email.md`)
- **Playbook:** `.cowork/segments/segment_b_handwerk.md`

## REVENUE MODEL

**Option A — Small Retainer:** €400–800/month (1-2 automations, <5 staff)  
**Option B — Standard Retainer:** €800–1,500/month (3-5 automations, 5–15 staff)  
**Option C — Growth Retainer:** €1,500–2,500/month (full stack, >15 staff, custom integrations)  
**Setup Fee:** €500–1,500 one-time (covers onboarding + first month customization)

Target mix: 60% Option A/B + 40% Option B/C → avg ~€750/month/client × 30 clients = €22.5K/month (conservative).
