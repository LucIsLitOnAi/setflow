## DIRECT RESEARCH PROCESS (Luc to do in Week 1)

**Step 1 — Google Maps Research (30 min per Gewerk)**
1. Search: "[Gewerk] Lübeck" on Google Maps
2. Look for businesses with: 4.0–4.6 rating, 20–80 reviews, no "booking" buttons, basic website
3. Note: business name, phone number (in listing), website URL
4. Score their digital presence (1–5 using scale from gastro_target_list.md)

**Step 2 — Website Check (5 min per business)**
- Does it look modern? (updated within last 2 years)
- Is there any online booking/job request form?
- Is there an email address? (signals responsiveness)
- Are they active on social media?

**Step 3 — Quick Call (7 min)**
- Not a sales call — a "qualifying" call
- Ask: "Haben Sie gerade ein Auftragsmanagement-System oder läuft das noch über Telefon und Zettel?"
- Listen: if they say "Telefon und Zettel" → hot lead, book discovery call
- If they say "Ja, wir haben [Software X]" → note it, lower priority
